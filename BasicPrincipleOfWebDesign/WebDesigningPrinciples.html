9 Basic Principles in Web Designing

  • Website Purpose
      • Describe Expertise
      Building your Reputation
      Generating Leads
      Sales and After Care
  • Simplicity
      • 1. Color
      Find a color palette that fits your brand
      Keep the color selection limited (less than 5 colors). Best practice is to use at least 3 - 4 colors
      2. Type
      Only use a maximum of 3 different fonts
      3. Imagery
      Use high quality images
  • Navigation
      • It is the way finding system used on websites where visitors interact and find what they are looking for.
      Make sure the navigation is not confusing
      Keep the navigation,simple,intuitive,and consistent
  • F-Shaped Pattern Reading
      • Eye tracking studies have found that most of what people see is in the top and left area of the screen.
      The F'shaped layout mimics our natural pattern of reading in the West(left to right and top to bottom).
  • Visual Hierarchy
      • Visual hierarchy is the arrangement of elements in order of importance.
      Establish a focal point to show visitors the most important information.
  • Content
      • An effective web design has both great design and great content
      Use compelling language
      Check basic grammar
      Content should be error free
      Not plagiarized
  • Grid Based Layout
      • Grids help to structure your design and keep your content organized.
      Arranges content into:
      -Clean rigid grid
      -Structure with columns
      -Sections that line up
      -Makes it feel balanced and ordered
  • Load Time
      • Waiting time is very essential for visitors
      Optimizing image sizes will help you load your site faster
  • Mobile Friendly
