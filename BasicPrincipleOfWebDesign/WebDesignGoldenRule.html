Five Golden Rules for Web Designing

  • Customer Demographics
      • Target your customer for whom you want to create your website
  • Useful Content
      • What will be the purpose of the website?
  • Call To Action
      • If your website is not making good conversions, then there is something lacking
      It guides visitors to take an action with one click
  • Study your Competitors
      • Benchmark other websites
      Main goal is to come up with a unique site fit to the purpose and needs of the audience
  • Check and Balance
      • Maintenance and continuous study of your website should be also be in your checklist
